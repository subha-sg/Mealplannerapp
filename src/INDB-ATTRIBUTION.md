## Why INDB is Important for NaturaNest

### 1. **Culturally Appropriate**
- Standard international nutrition databases often lack Indian recipes
- INDB provides accurate data for dal, roti, sabzi, curry, etc.
- Considers Indian cooking methods (tempering, pressure cooking, etc.)

### 2. **Life-Stage Specific**
- Data helps identify recipes suitable for:
  - Pregnancy and postpartum care
  - Perimenopause and menopause
  - Children's growth and development
  - Women over 50

### 3. **Regional Diversity**
- Covers North, South, East, West Indian cuisines
- Traditional recipes from different states
- Festival and special occasion foods

### 4. **Medical Conditions**
- Helps create diets for:
  - Diabetes management
  - Iron deficiency (anemia)
  - Calcium deficiency (osteoporosis)
  - High blood pressure
  - Weight management

## Data Usage Guidelines

### For Users:
- Nutritional values are approximate and may vary based on:
  - Ingredient quality
  - Cooking methods
  - Portion sizes
  - Regional variations
- Use values as guidelines, not absolute measures
- Consult healthcare providers for specific dietary needs

### For Developers:
- Always cite INDB as the data source
- Maintain data accuracy and precision
- Update data periodically from official sources
- Provide clear attribution in the application
