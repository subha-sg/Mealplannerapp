## Health Statistics (India)

### Why This App Matters
- **53% of Indian women** are anemic (WHO)
- **51% of pregnant women** suffer from iron deficiency
- **42% of children under 5** are stunted due to malnutrition
- Indian women have **earlier menopause onset** (age 46) vs. Western women (age 51)
- **1 in 3 Indian women over 50** has osteoporosis
- India has the **2nd highest diabetes population** globally

### Nutritional Gaps
Indian women typically consume:
- Only **50% of recommended iron** (need: 21mg, consume: ~10mg)
- Only **40% of recommended calcium** (need: 1000mg, consume: ~400mg)
- Insufficient protein during pregnancy and lactation
- Low vitamin D levels (affecting 70-90% of population)
